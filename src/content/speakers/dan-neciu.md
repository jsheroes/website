Neciu Dan is a seasoned engineering leader with over 15 years of expertise in the tech industry. Most recently, he served as the technical co-founder and Staff Engineer of CareerOS, where he spearheaded the development, maintenance, and scaling of the main application. Prior to that, Dan served as a Senior Product Engineer at the New York e-commerce brand AdoreMe and as a Senior Software Engineer at the food delivery company Glovo.

Today, Dan is the host of the very popular Señors at Scale podcast, organizer of ReactJS Barcelona, and creator of the international best-selling workshop Lizard to Wizard. He is also an esteemed international speaker and mentor, passionately encouraging best development practices with a keen focus on testing, accessibility, and security. His speaking engagements include React Alicante, React Rally, React Paris, React Summit, Utah JS, Techorama Belgium, JSWorld Amsterdam, CityJS Athens, CityJS Singapore, DevBCN, Talent Arena Barcelona, and Frontmania Netherlands.
